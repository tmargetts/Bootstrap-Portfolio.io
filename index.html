About Me

My name is Travis Margetts and I currently live in the great city of Austin, TX. My mother is a native Texan, while my father is a fellow Canadian. Like most great American imports I was made in Taiwan. I am an avid outdoorsman, wine enthusiast, amateur chef, animal lover, and overall gentlemen and scholar. Recently, in November of 2017, I got married Braveheart style on the Isle of Skye in Scotland to the love of my life Jordan Roman. Together, we have two fur children, Blu and Rose, who are Australian Shepherds. Unfortunately, they could not attend the wedding due to passport related issues. The officiant brought his own dog Tanni, which was a welcome addition to our wedding party.

Currently, I work at Blue Moon Software, as a technical support representative. My duties are resolving issues including but not limited to the use of the software product, knowledge of content, and third-party integration. However, when I am not helping old people download Google Chrome in order to navigate the all too confusing interweb, I prefer exploring Austin's outdoors and it's food scene.
